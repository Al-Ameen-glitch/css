Understanding Artificial Intelligence

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on creating machines capable of performing tasks that usually require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and natural language understanding.

How AI Works (Summary)

AI systems learn from massive datasets using complex algorithms. They identify patterns, make decisions, and improve themselves over time. Examples include facial recognition, language translation, and personalized content recommendations.
